Kanchan is working as a Tech Lead (AI Lab) at TikTok(Bytedance), Singapore. TikTok is a social media platform for creating, sharing and discovering short videos. Kanchan is part of AI-Lab and leading a small team for various projects. Kanchan is mainly responsible for all the projects related to deduplication system, search engine and part of recommendation system. There are a lot of users on TikTok who copy videos which increases negative posting expectations for other users and jeopardize the foundation of platform originality. Kanchan built a deduplication system in TikTok which scans billions of videos on a daily basis and detect tens of millions videos as duplicate and non-original contnet on the platform. TikTok stop those non-original content recommending to other user's feed which has a direct impact on the revenue of company.
Prior to TikTok, he worked at Shopee, a Singapore based e-commerce platform where he mostly worked on image segmentation and object detection and built a tool for Shopee product image segmentation which outperforms Alibaba Luban a strong competitor for Shopee. Kanchan also working on e-commerce product type detection for thousands of product types. It's a very challenging task as the number of classes is huge and beyond any traditional object detection method.
Kanchan has overall ~7 years of mix experience in the field of computer vision and natural language processing. His research interests include computer vision and image processing algorithms, such as Image Segmentation, Object Detection, Deduplication System, Recommender Systems, Search Engines.
